Discover Sierra Leone

Quick Facts

  • Located in West Africa
  • Capital: Freetown
  • Population: ~8 million
  • Official language: English
  • Independence: April 27, 1961
  • Famous for white sand beaches and diamonds

Famous Landmarks

  • Tacugama Chimpanzee Sanctuary: Home to rescued chimps
  • Bunce Island: Historic slave trade site
  • Outamba-Kilimi National Park: Diverse wildlife sanctuary
